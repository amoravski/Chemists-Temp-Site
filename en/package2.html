Optimal Safe Load and Geometry for Layered
Nanocomposites Under Thermomechanical Loading

This project was implemented with the financial support of Scientific Research Fund under contract № КП 06-Н57/3/15.11.2021

Work Package 2

Work Package 2 – Fundamental theoretical studies for obtaining the optimal safe intervals of external loads and the optimal geometry of combined loaded three-layer nanocomposites, by multiparameter optimization with genetic algorithms.

Leader of Work Package 2: Assoc. Prof. Dr. Elisaveta Georgieva Kirilova, Institute of Chemical Engineering – BAS
