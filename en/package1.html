Optimal Safe Load and Geometry for Layered
Nanocomposites Under Thermomechanical Loading

This project was implemented with the financial support of Scientific Research Fund under contract № КП 06-Н57/3/15.11.2021

Work Package 1

Work Package 1 – Fundamental theoretical research for analytical modelling and determination of stresses, strains and the causes of interfacial delamination in three-layer structures under combined loading, using a two-dimensional analytical model (stress-function variational method).

Leader of Work Package 1: Assoc. Prof. Dr. Tatyana Stefanova Petrova, Institute of Chemical Engineering – BAS
